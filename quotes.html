Dr.B.R . Ambedkar Quotes
  1. Unlike a drop of water which loses its identity when it joins the ocean, man does not lose his being in the society in which he lives.


  2. Man's life is independent. He is born not for the development of the society alone, but for the development of his self.


  3. The relationship between husband and wife should be one of closest friends.


  4. Every man who repeats the dogma of Mill that one country is no fit to rule another country must admit that one class is not fit to rule another class.


  5. Political tyranny is nothing compared to the social tyranny and a reformer who defies society is a more courageous man than a politician who defies Government.


  6. Men are mortal. So are ideas.


  7. An idea needs propagation as much as a plant needs watering. Otherwise both will wither and die.


  8. Law and order are the medicine of the body politic and when the body politic gets sick, medicine must be administered.


  9. I measure the progress of a community by the degree of progress which women have achieved.
